LA Hounds is a collective of dope Emcees
from various backgrounds who've
come together to form a Hip Hop supergroup.
Together they form an alliance whose fusion
of sounds and styles can match
any mood and cover any topic. They continue
to evolve their lyrics, cadence,
and delivery in order to create unique and
interesting music which can be
both fun and thought provoking at the same
time. True masters of their
craft, they bring raw emotion and energy to
their live performances
which results in an unforgettable experience.
