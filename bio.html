WordPlay is the music group breathing new
life into the modern Hip-Hop game. Bosky,
Chris, Eatz, and Jamal deliver unrestrained
lyrics with unique cadences, done in a way
that elevates and brings back respect to the
genre. Together they form an alliance in which
their fusion of styles successfully combines
old school lyrical talent with new school
beats and sounds, creating bangers unparalleled
in the music scene. True artists, WordPlay
brings raw emotion and energy to each venue,
resulting in an unforgettable experience.
